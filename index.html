IOT (internet of thinks)==>info


the Internet of Things (IoT) describes the network of physical objects - "things" or objects - that are integrated with sensors, software, and other technology for the purpose of connecting and exchanging data with people. other devices and systems on the Internet.

things have evolved due to the convergence of multiple technologies, real-time analytics, machine learning, commodity sensors, and embedded systems. The traditional areas of embedded systems, wireless sensor networks, control systems, automation (including home automation and home automation) and others all contribute to enabling the Internet of Things. In the consumer market, IoT technology is most often synonymous with products that fall under the concept of "smart home", including appliances and appliances (such as light fixtures, thermostats, systems and cameras). home security and other home appliances) that support one or more common ecosystems and can be controlled through devices associated with that ecosystem, such as smartphones and smart speakers. IoT can also be used in healthcare systems.
